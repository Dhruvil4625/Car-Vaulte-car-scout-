Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, selling, and test-driving vehicles on our platform.

Listing your car is quick and easy. Simply navigate to the Sell Car section from the main menu, create a new listing, and provide details such as your vehicle's make, model, year, and condition. Don't forget to upload high-quality photos to attract more buyers!

Our search engine uses standardized Indian price bands to make finding your perfect car easier. You can filter inventory using ranges such as Under ₹ 5 Lakh, ₹ 5–10 Lakh, up to Above ₹ 1 Cr. Just select your preferred bracket in the search widget.

When you find a car you like, click the "Schedule Test Drive" button on the listing page. Buyers can request their preferred dates, and our staff or the private seller will coordinate and confirm the schedule directly through the platform's internal dashboard.
